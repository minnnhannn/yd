    企業研究方法 Business Research Methods (6130)，3學分

    Course Description課程敘述:
    This course is compulsory for the executive MBA students, and mainly about the fundamental concepts and applications of research methods in the business and management fields. This course also covers the introduction of library database, statistical analysis and results explanation, and students are expected to use research design and strategy to develop a research proposal. The research methods taught in this course includes survey, case studies, interview, qualitative research, quantitative research, experimental design, observational studies, focused group and secondary research methods. In addition, the statistical analysis and regressions, and SPSS are introduced. The library database and information searching are introduced and the library visit is also held. Finally, we apply case studies and exercises to teach students to apply appropriate research methods to solve the problems in practices.

    本課程為MBA的必修課程，主要教授企業管理的研究方法的基本概念和運用，並介紹圖書資料庫的使用以及量化的統計分析與結果解釋，最後學生能融合研究設計與研究策略，發展一研究計畫書。本課程將介紹各類型的研究方法，包括調查研究法、個案研究、訪談法、質化、量化研究、實驗設計、觀察法、焦點團隊、次集資料法等。本課程也將介紹圖書館資料庫使用與資料搜尋、實際操作與圖書館參訪。最後，運用個案討論與演練等方式，期許學生能更深入地了解實務經理人如何運用研究方法來解決實務上的管理問題。最後，學生將採用適當的研究設計提出一研究計畫書。

    Course Objectives課程目標:
    This course aims to educate students with the following abilities.
    1. Understanding the research methods in fundamental concept, advantages and disadvantages, and research design (recognition),
    2. Nurturing the proper attitude of the researchers (emotion),
    3. Having the abilities of statistical analysis and explanation (skill)
    4. Developing the ability in research proposal (skill), and
    5. Developing the ability to use research approaches to solve the problems in both of practice and theory (skill)

    本課程的目標在於教育學生關於研究的概念、流程與設計、資料蒐集、設計問卷、資料分析與解釋、以及研究計畫書的撰寫。本課程教育學生：
    1. 瞭解研究方法的基本概念、流程和設計(認知)
    2. 培養研究者應有的正確態度(情意)
    3. 具有統計分析資料與解釋的能力(技能)
    4. 撰寫研究計畫書的能力(技能)
    5. 運用研究方法來探討實務或理論上的研究問題(技能)

    Company survey企業調研:
    企業調研為團隊的田野調查，學生必須到指定的公司進行消費者行為的實地觀察與訪談。學生必須事先設計訪談的問題(※開放性問題)，並且進行前測，將問卷修改到能真正符合消費者的實際情況。在實際的企業調研過程中，學生需著正式服裝訪談、拍攝當天調研的實際概況，並於隔周進行口頭報告，內容須包括調研公司的整體環境、質性訪談結果整理、重要錄音播放、結果分析等，請不要做量化的統計資料或量表。每組報告為25分鐘。

    Proposal Presentations:
    本研究計畫書為個人作業。每位學生必須於2014年12/18、12/25，1/9日進行1分鐘的口頭報告，報告須使用PPT，至多不能超過12頁，內容須包括研究題目、研究問題的重要性與研究目的、關鍵的文獻探討(一個理論即可)、研究方法(資料蒐集與研究對象)、預期研究貢獻與價值。

    Course Description  課程敘述:
    This course is elective in the MBA in technology management program. This course mainly applies the important theories related to technology and society including technology determination and social shaping of technology theories to explore the mutual shaping process of technology, humanity and society. This course consists of important theories related to technology and society, technology environment and innovation, technology and entrepreneurship, technology and cultural creativity, technology and digital archives, technology and creative city, second machine age and human value.

    本課程為科技管理碩士班的必選修課程。本課程主要是以科技與社會的基本理論(包括科技決定論與科技的社會形塑論)來探討科技、人文與社會的相互形塑與發展的過程。課程內容包括科技決定論理論、科技的社會形塑理論、科技消費者行為、科技環境與創新、科技與創業、科技與組織創新、科技人文與創意、科技與數位典藏、科技與創意城市、第二次機器時代與人文價值等。

    w Understand the important issues related to technology, environment and innovation (cognition).
    w Understand the mutual shaping and mutual influence of technology and humanity (cognition).
    w Understand the mutual shaping and development of technology and society (cognition).
     
    本課程的目標本課程的目標在於培養學生下列的能力:

    創意思考與創意領導(Creative Thinking and Creative Leadership，6129) (3 Credits)
    教室Classroom：R319
    上課時間：Thursday 9:10-12:00 noon
    授課老師： 陳明惠教授

    課程簡述: Course Description
    本課程主要教授創意心智模式、創意思維與運用、創意與領導以及科技商品化與行銷四部分。第一部份的創意心智模式，內容涵蓋創意巨人、心智慣性與創意學說。第二部分的創意思維與運用，內容涵蓋六項創意思維、六項創新運用與創意問題思解法。第三部分的團隊創意管理，內容涵蓋團隊競合管理、團隊創意因子、創意領導與保護創意智財權。第四部份為文創產業與文創園區的發展與管理，內容包括文創產業的定義與內涵、全球文創產業的概況以及成功文創園區的發展與管理。第五部分為創意專案報告，內容涵蓋成功且創新的科技產品、服務、娛樂或休閒活動的範疇。

    (Eng.) This course is elective in the graduate program. This course consists of five parts taught in this course including creative mindset, creative thinking and application, creative team leadership, cultural and creative industry, and creativity and technology. Students are expected to apply the knowledge and skills taught in this course to both of the work and life, implement the creative technology and service projects, and conduct field study to the cultural and creative quarters to investigate their current situations and challenges. Through the teaching approaches including lecture, group discussion, case study, movie analysis and field study, students are expected to acquire professional and diverse knowledge in the given domain.

    Course Description/課程簡述:
    This course studies the process of entrepreneurship and its central role in new and emerging ventures. This course will give an introduction to how new enterprises are developed by applying entrepreneurship, creativity and strategic planning. The entrepreneurship education, entrepreneurial mind and creativity, opportunity recognition, design thinking, the resources of developing a business plan, the process of new venture development, and team building, e-business, case studies and entrepreneurial project are covered in this course.

    本課程主要以教授創業經營的過程以及新事業的發展歷程。本課程主要是介紹如何運用創業精神、創意和策略思維來發展新事業。本課程的主要教授主題包括創業教育、創業心智與創業創意力、創業機會辨識、設計思考概念與演練、創業計劃書的撰寫、創業歷程、創業團隊合作演練、個案分析與討論、以及創業專案的實作。

    Course Objectives/課程目標:
    Having completed this course, students are able to understand the process of entrepreneurship, to develop the skills of making business plans, to develop team building capability, and to have the capability of completing the entrepreneurial project.
    透過本課程，學生可以了解創業歷程、撰寫創業計劃書、運用設計思考的技巧與能力、建構團隊合作、以及實作並完成創業專案。
